UEFA AUCTIONS

The UEFA Auction was where the biggest football fans and economics geeks came together for an epic showdown! Members got a chance to put their financial prowess to the test by participating in mock auctions of football players and teams. From Messi to Ronaldo, and Liverpool to Real Madrid, no player or team was safe from the bidding wars! The competition was fierce, the strategies were clever, and the excitement was palpable! But it wasn't just about the thrill of the auction - the event also served as a platform for members to learn about economic concepts such as transfer pricing, player valuation, and team strategies in the world of football. So whether you were a die-hard football fanatic or a curious economics enthusiast, the UEFA Auction was the place to be for an unforgettable evening of intellectual stimulation and entertainment!
