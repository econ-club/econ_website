Our President

She is one of the most well-educated professors in our college. Her qualifications include an MA, an MPhil in Economics, and a PhD in Industrial Management. She is a professor at Thapar University, School of Humanities and Social Sciences, specializing in Industrial Management, Behavioral Economics, Data Analytics, Entrepreneurship, and IPRs.
